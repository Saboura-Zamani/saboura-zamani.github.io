Research positions

PhD Researcher — University of Szczecin
Institute of Physics, University of Szczecin, Poland · 2023–Present

PhD candidate in Gravitation and Cosmology under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Hab. Vincenzo Salzano. My thesis, "Probing the nature of dark matter with modified Poisson's equations", focuses on testing non-minimally coupled dark matter models and disformal coupling theories against observations of clusters of galaxies, gravitational lensing, and ultra-diffuse galaxies. Funded by the NCN Preludium Bis 3 grant (2021/43/O/ST9/00664).

Visiting Researcher — University of Salamanca
University of Salamanca, Spain · January–April 2026

Three-month research stay with the Cosmology group at the University of Salamanca, supported by the NAWA Preludium Bis3 mobility grant. The visit focused on collaborative work on modified gravity models and dark matter phenomenology.

M.Sc. Researcher — Golestan University
Golestan University, Iran · 2018–2021

Completed my M.Sc. in Gravitation and Cosmology under the supervision of Dr. Siamak Akhshabi. My thesis, "Gravitational Lensing by a black hole in non-Riemannian space-times", investigated gravitational lensing in Poincaré gauge theories of gravity, leading to two peer-reviewed publications.
