Community Involvement

Alongside my research, I am actively involved in community-building initiatives in astronomy and related sciences. I am a mentee and webinar coordinator at the Supernova Foundation, an international initiative that supports women and underrepresented groups in astronomy.

Through this role, I contribute to mentorship activities, help coordinate scientific webinars, and support outreach efforts that connect students and early-career researchers with academic opportunities. This experience has strengthened my interest in science communication, inclusive research communities, and creating supportive international networks for young scientists.
