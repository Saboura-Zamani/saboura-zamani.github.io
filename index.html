I am a PhD candidate in gravitation and cosmology at the University of Szczecin, working with Prof. Vincenzo Salzano. My research focuses on the phenomenology of dark matter and modified gravity, with particular emphasis on modified Poisson equations, non-minimally coupled dark matter and gravitational lensing.
